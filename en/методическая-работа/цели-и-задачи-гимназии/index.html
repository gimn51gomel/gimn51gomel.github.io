GOALS AND OBJECTIVES OF ACTIVITIES of the State Educational Institution "Gymnasium No. 51 of Gomel" for the 2022/2023 academic year

Problem - Improving the quality of education through the personal and social development of each student, improving the system of pedagogical tools to achieve maximum results in teaching and educating students.

Goal - Creating conditions for the successful functioning and development of an educational institution aimed at developing computer literacy of teachers in a digital educational environment and ensuring an increase in the efficiency and quality of education.

Tasks:

  • ensure the improvement of the quality of education through: the formation of students’ meta-subject skills and abilities through the introduction by teachers of modern educational trends, in particular, the approaches that are implemented in the tasks of the international study of the quality of education PISA; ensuring the relationship of training and education through the implementation of the educational potential of educational, optional classes and classes during extracurricular time; implementation of STEAM approaches in the process of education and upbringing; implementation of correction and regulation of the educational process, taking into account the results of republican monitoring;

  • to promote the formation of a diversified, morally mature, creative personality of students based on moral values ​​aimed at creating a sense of patriotism, citizenship, national identity based on state ideology through: using the potential of the sixth school day, holding thematic Saturdays; conducting educational excursions in order to form respect for the cultural and historical heritage and traditions of the Belarusian people, the activities of the “School of an Active Citizen” and the education of a respectful attitude towards the state, its symbols, ideology, improving interaction with legal representatives by achieving unity of views, goals and objectives of education;

  • contribute to the improvement of the professional competence of teachers by mastering the methods of organizing student learning with the widespread use of modern means of communication; to assist in the development and implementation in the educational process of effective pedagogical experience, modern educational trends in the use of visual tools in the educational process;

  • improve and strengthen the material and technical base, the information space of the gymnasium by attracting funds from non-budgetary sources, create safe conditions for the participants in the educational process and form healthy lifestyle skills.

Methodological theme “Improving the professional competence of teachers in the formation of personal, meta-subject and subject competencies of students”.
